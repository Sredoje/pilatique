The history of reformer

The Pilates reformer was developed by Joseph Pilates, a German physical trainer, in the early 20th century. Originally designed as a rehabilitation tool, it has since evolved into a popular apparatus used for both rehabilitation and fitness purposes, gaining recognition for its effectiveness in improving strength, flexibility, and body alignment.

The Pilates reformer has become a staple in Pilates studios worldwide, revolutionizing the way people approach exercise and providing a versatile platform for achieving a balanced and healthy body.
